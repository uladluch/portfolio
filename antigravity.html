Connect Gemini CLI

Bridge your mobile app with Gemini CLI on your computer.

1. Open Terminal
Install Gemini CLI (if needed)
npm i -g @google/gemini-cli
Sign in to your Google account (if needed)
gemini
2. Start the bridge
npx antigravity-gemini-bridge@latest
3. Tap Connect below
