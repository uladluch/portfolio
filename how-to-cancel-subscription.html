How to Cancel Your Subscription

These steps apply to subscriptions purchased via the App Store on your iPhone or iPad.
  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. Tap your name at the top to access your Apple ID settings.
  3. Select Subscriptions.
  4. Find and tap on SnapTasty in the list of active subscriptions.
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription and confirm your choice.
After cancellation, your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

For more details, visit Apple’s official guide: How to cancel a subscription from Apple.
