Featured work

Building a Calm Banking Companion

I helped overworked freelancers reconcile multi-bank cash flow in minutes by rethinking notifications, IA, and automation paths.

Framing the Problem

User interviews revealed that busy freelancers struggled to reconcile invoices across multiple banks. They needed a calm assistant that clarified cash flow in seconds.

Design Principles

I worked with the founding team to define success, streamline notifications, and create a flexible IA that reduced noise while keeping high-signal events visible.

Outcome

The pilot release helped early adopters reconcile their statements 2× faster, freeing time for client work and paving the way for automated guidance.
