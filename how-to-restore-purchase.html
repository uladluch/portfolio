How to Restore a Purchase

If you have previously purchased a subscription or one-time feature in SnapTasty, you can restore your purchase at any time.
  1. Open the SnapTasty app on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. Go to the Settings or Account section.
  3. Tap the Restore Purchase button.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions. You may be asked to sign in with your Apple ID.
  5. Once restored, your premium features or subscription will be reactivated.
If you experience any issues, please make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

For more help, see Apple’s guide: Restore your in-app purchases.
